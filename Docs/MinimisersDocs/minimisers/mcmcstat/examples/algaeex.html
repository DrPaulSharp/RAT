0016 %%
0017 % This is a simplified lake algae dynamics model. We consider
0018 % phytoplankton _A_, zooplankton _Z_ and nutrition _P_
0019 % (eg. phosphorus) available for _A_ in the water. The system is
0020 % affected by the water outflow/inflow _Q_, incoming phosphorus load
0021 % _Pin_ and temperature _T_. It is described as a simple
0022 % predator - pray dynamics between _A_ and _Z_. The growth of _A_ is
0023 % limited by the availability of _P_ and it depends on the water
0024 % temperature _T_. The inflow/outflow _Q_ affects both _A_ and _P_,
0025 % but not _Z_. We use the following equations:
0026
